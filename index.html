Prime Minister's Employment Generation Programme (PMEGP)

Eligibility Criteria

  • Age: Any individual above 18 years.
  • Income: No income ceiling for assistance.
  • Education: Minimum VIII standard pass for projects above ₹10 lakh.
  • New Projects: Only new projects are eligible.
