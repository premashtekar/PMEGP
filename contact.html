National Level

KVIC: Khadi and Village Industries Commission-The Khadi and Village Industries Commission (KVIC) is a statutory body under the Ministry of Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises. Established in 1956, KVIC aims to promote and develop khadi and village industries, generating employment and income for rural artisans and entrepreneurs, while preserving traditional crafts.

State Level

Contact your State KVIC Directorates or District Industries Centres (DICs)-District Industries Centre (DIC) is a government agency responsible for promoting and developing industries at the district level. DICs provide various services.DICs play a crucial role in implementing government schemes, such as PMEGP, to promote industrial growth and employment generation in rural and urban areas.
