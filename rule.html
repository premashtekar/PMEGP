PMEGP Guidelines

Objectives

  • Generate sustainable employment opportunities in rural and urban areas.
  • Support micro-enterprises through financial assistance.
  • Promote entrepreneurship among youth and artisans.
  • Reduce migration by providing employment in local areas.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Any individual above 18 years of age.
  • For projects above ₹10 lakh (Manufacturing) and ₹5 lakh (Service), at least an VIII standard pass is required.
  • Only new projects are eligible.
  • Self-Help Groups, Co-operative Societies, and Trusts are eligible.
  • Existing units and businesses already availing government benefits are not eligible.

Financial Assistance

  • Maximum Project Cost: ₹25 lakhs (Manufacturing) / ₹10 lakhs (Service).
  • Subsidy: Up to 35% for rural and 25% for urban areas.
  • Beneficiary’s own contribution: 5-10% of the project cost.
  • Bank provides the remaining loan component.
